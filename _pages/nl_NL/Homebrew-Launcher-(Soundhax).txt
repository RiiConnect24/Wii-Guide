De Homebrew Launcher heeft veel verschillende entrypoints of lanceringsmethoden.
{: .notice}

Soundhax is compatible with versions 9.0.0 through 11.2.0 in the EUR, JPN, KOR, and USA regions.
{: .notice--info}

#### Benodigdheden

+ De Homebrew [Starter Kit](http://smealum.github.io/ninjhax2/starter.zip)
+ The latest release of [Soundhax](http://soundhax.com/) *(for your device and region)*
+ De [otherapp payload](https://smealum.github.io/3ds/#otherapp) *(voor jouw 3DS model en regio)*

#### Instructies

1. Kopieer _de inhoud_ van de 'starter'-map in 'starter.zip' naar de root van je SD-Kaart
2. Copy the Soundhax `.m4a` to the root of your SD card
3. Kopieer de otherapp payload naar de root van je SD kaart en hernoem deze naar`otherapp.bin`
4. Stop je SD-Kaart terug in je 3DS
5. Start Nintendo 3DS Sound
6. Doorloop alle tips die de vogel je geeft, sluit en open de app daarna opnieuw
  + Launching Soundhax immediately would cause these tips to appear on every launch of the Nintendo 3DS Sound until this is done
7. Go to `/SDCARD`, then play "<3 nedwill 2016"
  + This may take many tries
  + If it freezes, just force the console to power off by holding the power button, then try again
8. Your console should load the Homebrew Launcher
