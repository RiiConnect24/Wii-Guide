#### What ye need

* The latest release of [Decrypt9WIP](https://github.com/d0k3/Decrypt9WIP/releases/latest/)
* The latest release of [safehax](https://github.com/TiniVi/safehax/releases/latest)
* The latest release of [fasthax](https://github.com/nedwill/fasthax/releases/latest)

#### Instructions

1. Remove your SD card from your device, leaving it on the Homebrew Launcher, then insert your SD card into your computer
2. Create a folder named `files9` on th' root 'o ye SD card if it does not already exist
3. Copy `safehax.3dsx` to th' `/3ds/` folder on ye SD card
4. Copy `fasthax.3dsx` to th' `/3ds/` folder on ye SD card
5. Copy `Decrypt9WIP.bin` from th' Decrypt9WIP `.zip` to th' root 'o ye SD card 'n rename `Decrypt9WIP.bin` to `safehaxpayload.bin`
6. Reinsert ye SD card into ye 3DS
7. Launch fasthax from the list of homebrew
  + You may have to scroll down to see this option
8. Once it has completed, press (Start) to exit back to the Homebrew Launcher
  + 'Tis may take several tries
  + If it freezes, just force the console to power off by holding the power button, then try again
9. Launch safehax from the list of homebrew
  + You may have to scroll down to see this option
  + If it freezes, just force the console to power off by holding the power button, then try again
10. If th' exploit was successful, ye gunna have booted into Decrypt9
