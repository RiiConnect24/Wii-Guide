Homebrew Launcher mempunyai pelbagai cara masuk, atau cara melancarkannya.
{: .notice}

If you do not already own any of these games and do not have another 3DS that can access the Homebrew Launcher, the cheapest option is to buy a "Nintendo Selects" copy of [Ocarina of Time 3D](https://amzn.to/2fkaKdp) (ensure you get the correct cartridge for your region) and a [Powersaves](https://amzn.to/2fb3VY7) (compatible with all regions), then use oot3dhax from the chart below.
{: .notice--info}

Ensure your device's Wireless Communication is turned on as udsploit (used in the next page) will need the wireless module to be active to function, and some devices (New 3DS and 2DS) cannot adjust the Wireless Communication setting from the Homebrew Launcher. Wireless Communication only has to be on; connecting it to an access point is not required.
{: .notice--info}

### Apa yang anda perlukan

* Homebrew [Starter Kit](http://smealum.github.io/ninjhax2/starter.zip)

#### Arahan

1. Power off your device
1. Pasang kad SD anda ke dalam komputer
1. Salin _kandungan_ `starter.zip` letakkan di root kad SD anda

1. Reinsert your SD card into your device
1. Power on your device
1. Use one of the following alternate entrypoints to get into the Homebrew Launcher:
